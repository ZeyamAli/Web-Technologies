PUGC History

University of the Punjab, Gujranwala Campus (also referred to as Punjab University Gujranwala campus or PUGC) is a satellite campus of University of the Punjab, Lahore which is located in Gujranwala, Punjab, Pakistan. It is the third-oldest campus amongst the five University of the Punjab campuses. The university occupies 81 kanals (approx. 10.03 acres) located near the Ali Pur Chowk, Rawalpindi bypass, Gujranwala. The University offers undergraduate, graduate and post graduate programs in six main departments.

Why PUGC Works

PUGC works by fostering an innovative learning environment, combining academic rigor with practical experience. Our personalized approach equips students with the skills needed for success in their chosen fields.

Personalized Learning

At PUGC, we prioritize personalized learning experiences that cater to each student's unique strengths and interests. Through adaptive teaching methods and tailored academic support, we ensure that students receive individual attention to help them thrive. Our faculty utilizes technology and data-driven insights to design learning paths that align with both personal and professional goals, empowering students to succeed in their chosen fields.

Our Philosphy

At PUGC, we focus on fostering innovation, critical thinking, and holistic development. Our commitment to blending theoretical knowledge with practical application empowers students to reach their full potential. We aim to cultivate a culture of collaboration and ethical responsibility, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for their careers and ready to positively impact society.

PUGC Principle

At PUGC, we uphold integrity, innovation, and inclusivity, fostering a diverse community that values all perspectives. Our focus on academic rigor and practical learning equips students with essential skills for success. We emphasize collaboration and ethical responsibility, preparing graduates to be conscientious leaders and active contributors to society.

Key of Success

Success at PUGC is built on continuous learning, resilience, and collaboration. We foster a growth mindset, encouraging students to embrace challenges while emphasizing practical experience and effective communication. By creating a supportive community and promoting innovation, we empower students to achieve their goals and make a positive impact on society.
